Travel to Florida

2017, Dec 17    

This post is for travel plan of visiting Florida, including Miami and Orlando.

Day1 (Dec. 19th, Miami)

  • Flights from Houston / Los Angeles / Orlando / Atlanta / New York to Miami

Day2 (Dec. 20th, Miami)

  • Little Havana
  • Miami Beach or South Beach

Day3 & Day4 (Dec. 21st & 22nd, Key West)

  • Drive from Miami to Key West (3 hours)
  • Drive along US-1
  • Ernest Hemingway House, Southernmost Point, sunset on Mallory Square, etc.
  • Drive from Key West to Miami

Day5 (Dec. 23rd, Miami)

  • Everglades National Park
  • Flight from Miami to Orlando

Day6 (Dec. 24th, Orlando)

  • Walt Disney World Resort

Day7 (Dec. 25th, Orlando)

  • Universal Studios Florida

Day8 (Dec. 26th, Orlando)

  • SeaWorld Orlando

Day9 (Dec. 27th, Orlando)

  • Flights from Orlando to Houston / Los Angeles / Atlanta / New York
